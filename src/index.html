ABOUT Ymetis

We transform businesses at scale with our wide range of managed services …but we do so through a unique, co-development model.

WHY YMETIS

Through profit-sharing agreements, joint-ventures and BOT-models, our partnerships align our priorities entirely with those of our clients, thereby guaranteeing the kind of commitment that’s hard to find elsewhere.

COMBINED STRENGTHS

We combine our clients’ product or service knowledge and industry expertise with our own expertise in technology & analytics, so that they can finally benefit from their own data.

WHY INNOVATE

To seize the opportunities that come with digitization, leading consumer brands today must rethink the traditional ways of working, adopt an agile attitude and, most importantly, seek access to new skills.

COLLABORATIVE ADVANTAGE

We help brands on their transformation journey by complementing them with world-class technology and analytics talent, so that they can focus on what they do best – design, produce and market desirable products and services.

Services

Our services span program management, ecommerce solutions, organisation design, process engineering, technology support and more.

ECOMMERCE

We use rigorous, fact-based analyses to help companies define their ecommerce agendas, and constantly develop their teams and make hard choices on where to play and how to win.

ORGANIZATIONAL RE-DESIGN

A well-designed operating model allows companies turn strategy into results. We help companies clarify roles & responsibilities and create a customized organizational model in service of their ecommerce strategy

TECHNOLOGY SUPPORT

Our technology support services include testing support (test automation, UAT and SIT), Conversion Rate Optimization (CRO), Platform Integration Support and Master Data Management (MDM) services.

CONSUMER & PRODUCT INSIGHTS

Timely and relevant reporting and insights drive faster business and operational decisions and help create a culture of data-driven decision making. Our team of data engineering and visualization experts help accelerate time to insights by leveraging proven approaches, technology partnerships and past experience.

DIGITALISATION

As an experienced partner in the digital journey, we know what it takes. Bringing business owners and data & technology experts closer together, redesigning business processes, building new capabilities and redefining the roles & responsibilities.

PERSONALIZATION SERVICES

Once a consumer data foundation and technology tooling is in place, brands need to tailor and deliver a personalized, omnichannel consumer experience. We deliver advanced personalization and recommendation solutions through a combination of proprietary algorithms and third party services

data engineer

Lead role

Job descriptioN

We are looking to hire a Professionals with 6 – 10 years’ experience in Data Engineering with exposure to SAP HANA enterprise data warehousing. The candidate should have ability to lead point to point data integrations and a classic data warehouse solution building to a uniform data lake, enabling all downstream ETL and processes which involves WebSphere Commerce database ingestion, SAP HANA ETL, SQL expertise for access to Adobe and other support system.

Responsibilities

  • Independently lead data engineering work streams involving SAP HANA, SQL and other ECommerce support systems

  • Perform data architecting within the data lake for reporting specific views, joins, aggregation etc.

  • Connect with the required APIs to streamline the data into final Tableau dashboards

  • Discuss with the technical teams at client side (SAP consultants, IT, Business Intelligence teams) and solve data extraction and warehousing challenges and suggest feasible options

  • Communication with clients at operational level

  • Guiding medior team members and task supervision

Required experience

  • 6-10 years of professional work experience in the Data Engineering domain

  • Hands on exposure in ETL tools – Alteryx, KNime, SAP ETL etc

  • Data warehousing, Big Data, SAP HANA, SQL exposure

  • Experience working in Ecommerce domain is highly desirable

  • Exposure to data extraction from Adobe, Payment systems, Shipping systems

  • Basic understanding of BI tools and KPI reporting in Retail industry

  • Ability to assess performance of data pipelines for latency and query run times

  • Exposure working in an Agile environment

  • Willingness to be travel on a need basis
